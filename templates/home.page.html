Hi there! I am Zachary!
Welcome to Fort Smythe Bed and Breakfast, where comfort meets elegance in a charming historical setting. Nestled in the heart of picturesque Smytheville, our bed and breakfast offers a unique blend of classic sophistication and modern amenities, perfect for both leisurely getaways and night retreats.

Our Property: Fort Smythe is housed in a beautifully restored 19th-century estate, showcasing timeless architecture with modern touches. Our meticulously maintained grounds feature lush gardens, serene walking paths, and cozy outdoor seating areas. The interior exudes warmth and character, with vintage furnishings, hardwood floors, and inviting fireplaces.

Accommodations: Each of our five elegantly appointed guest rooms offers a restful haven with luxurious linens, plush bedding, and tasteful decor. Choose from our selection of rooms, including the Regency Suite with its grand four-poster bed, the Victorian Room with antique furnishings, or the Modern Loft for a contemporary touch. All rooms come equipped with complimentary Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and private en-suite bathrooms.

Dining: Start your day with our gourmet breakfast served in our charming dining room. Enjoy a variety of freshly prepared options including farm-to-table eggs, artisanal pastries, and seasonal fruit. For a special touch, indulge in our signature breakfast dishes like the Smytheville Benedict or the Fort Classic French Toast.
Experience the charm and hospitality of Fort Smythe Bed and Breakfast – where every stay is a journey back in time, complemented by modern comforts and exceptional service. We look forward to welcoming you to our historical retreat.
